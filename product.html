    Floral hoop (medium)

    £25.00

    Colour:

    Most floral hoops start with a quick photo on Areej’s phone – a bunch of flowers on a table, a bouquet from a friend, or colours she spots on a walk. She sketches the shapes lightly on the fabric, then spends the evening layering petals, leaves and tiny details until the hoop feels “full” but still soft.

    The medium size is designed to be easy: big enough to notice on a wall or shelf, but small enough to tuck into a gift bag or box. It works beautifully in bedrooms, hallways, reading corners or as a little highlight on a gallery wall.

    • Hand‑embroidered from scratch – every flower is stitched, not printed
    • Choose a colour palette to match your home or the person you’re gifting
    • Medium hoop size – simple to hang, lean or wrap as a present
    • Ideal for birthdays, Eid, housewarmings or “just because” gifts
