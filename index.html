How it works

Every hoop goes through a slow, careful process – from idea, to sketch, to the last stitch.

  1. You choose the design and personalise text or colours.
  2. We sketch the layout and select matching threads.
  3. Everything is embroidered by hand and neatly finished.
  4. Your hoop is wrapped and posted, ready to hang or gift.

About Areej Handmade

Areej Handmade began from a love of embroidery, home decor and thoughtful gifts. Each piece is created in small batches at home, with plenty of tea and pink thread.

If you have a specific idea (a phrase, room colours, or a theme), you can get in touch and we’ll see what we can create together.
