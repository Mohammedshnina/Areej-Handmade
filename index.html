How it Works

Each hoop is created slowly and carefully — from sketch, to stitching, to finishing.

  1. You choose the design and custom details.
  2. We sketch, select threads and embroider by hand.
  3. The hoop is finished neatly with backing and labels.
  4. Packed beautifully and shipped to you.
