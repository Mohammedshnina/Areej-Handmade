About Areej Handmade

A small family‑run embroidery studio creating thoughtful, personalised pieces to celebrate your home, your loved ones and your special moments.

Our story

Areej Handmade started as a quiet evening hobby and slowly grew into a tiny home‑studio. Every hoop is drawn, stitched and finished by hand – no machines, no shortcuts, just patience and a lot of love.

We believe that slow, handmade pieces carry a different kind of feeling. They’re not fast fashion: they’re keepsakes that move with you from one home to the next.

What we value

  • Thoughtful, meaningful gifts over mass‑produced decor
  • Colours that feel soft, warm and welcoming
  • Taking time with each order instead of rushing

Have a custom idea in mind? Email us or message on WhatsApp and we’ll see what we can create together.
